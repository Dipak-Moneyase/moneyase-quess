  • Containment of the Breach:
    • We will take immediate actions to contain the breach, preventing further exposure of your personal information.
    • Steps may include locking affected systems or accounts and isolating compromised data.
  • Assessment of Damage:
    • We will assess the extent of the damage and the specific data compromised during the breach.
    • We will evaluate any potential risks to your privacy and take steps to minimize those risks.
  • Preventing Future Occurrences:
    • After addressing the breach, we will implement measures to enhance our security infrastructure and reduce the likelihood of similar breaches occurring in the future.
